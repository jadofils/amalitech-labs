# Final Assessment – Agile & DevOps in Practice (Individual Test)

## Objective

Demonstrate your ability to independently apply Agile principles and DevOps practices to plan, execute, and iteratively deliver a working solution.

---

## Assessment Brief

You will individually design, plan, and deliver a small working prototype/service over two simulated sprints.

The emphasis is **not on the size of the product**, but on how effectively you apply Agile and DevOps practices throughout the process.

---

## Assessment Structure

### Sprint 0 – Planning (Setup)

- Define a product vision (1–2 sentences)
- Create a product backlog (minimum 5 user stories)
- Prioritize and estimate (story points or relative sizing)
- Define:
  - Acceptance Criteria
  - Definition of Done (DoD)
- Plan Sprint 1 (select 2–3 stories)

---

### Sprint 1 – Execution

- Deliver at least 2 backlog items
- Use version control (commit history must show iterative progress)
- Set up a CI pipeline (e.g., GitHub Actions, GitLab CI)
- Write unit and/or integration tests
- Produce:
  - Sprint Review (document/demo of delivered work)
  - Retrospective (include at least 2 improvements for Sprint 2)

---

### Sprint 2 – Execution & Improvement

- Apply improvements from Sprint 1 retrospective
- Deliver at least 2 additional backlog items
- Add basic monitoring/logging, such as:
  - Console logs
  - Error tracking
  - Health endpoint
- Produce:
  - Sprint Review
  - Retrospective (document lessons and improvements)

---

## Evaluation Criteria

| Dimension | Weight | What Good Looks Like |
|-----------|--------|----------------------|
| Agile Practice | 25% | Clear backlog, prioritization, acceptance criteria, and proper sprint planning |
| DevOps Practice | 25% | Functional CI/CD pipeline, integrated tests, basic monitoring/logging |
| Delivery Discipline | 20% | Commit history shows iterative development (no "big-bang" commits) |
| Prototype Quality | 20% | Working solution that meets acceptance criteria |
| Reflection | 10% | Retrospectives show meaningful improvements between Sprint 1 & 2 |

---

## Deliverables

Each trainee must submit:

**1. Backlog & Sprint Plans**
- User stories
- Estimates
- Definition of Done (DoD)
- Sprint breakdowns

**2. Codebase**
- With commit history showing incremental delivery

**3. CI/CD Evidence**
- Workflow files, screenshots, or logs

**4. Testing Evidence**
- Unit/integration test results

**5. Sprint Review Documents**
- Screenshots, demos, or short write-ups

**6. Retrospectives**
- Reflection on process improvements
